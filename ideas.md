<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Neo-Brutalism meets Swiss Style
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Raw Authenticity**: Unapologetic use of default system fonts, high contrast, and visible structural elements.
  2. **Information Density**: Layouts that prioritize content visibility without hiding behind abstractions.
  3. **Functional Decoration**: Visual elements that serve a purpose (guides, rulers, coordinates) rather than just ornamentation.
  4. **Hyper-Legibility**: Typography is treated as the primary interface element.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Monochrome Base**: Stark black (#0a0a0a) and white (#fafafa) for maximum contrast.
  - **Signal Colors**: Usage of "alert" colors like International Orange (#FF4F00) or Neon Green (#00FF00) for interactive elements, signifying "system status" or "action required."
  - **Intent**: To convey technical competence and a "no-nonsense" engineering approach.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Modular Grid**: Visible grid lines separating content blocks.
  - **Asymmetry**: Content blocks that span uneven columns to create rhythm.
  - **Sticky Elements**: Navigation and key information that sticks to edges, mimicking HUDs or terminal interfaces.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Marquee Text**: Scrolling text for announcements or skills.
  - **Visible Borders**: Thick, hard borders (2px-4px) for all containers.
  - **System Icons**: Use of raw SVG icons or pixelated icons.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Instant Feedback**: Hover states that snap instantly (0s transition) or use glitch effects.
  - **Cursor Effects**: Custom cursor that inverts colors or reveals underlying data.

  **Animation**:
  - **Staccato Motion**: Animations that step rather than flow (e.g., typewriter effect, frame-by-frame reveals).
  - **Glitch/Distortion**: Subtle chromatic aberration on interaction.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: 'Space Grotesk' or 'Chakra Petch' (Display, Uppercase, Tight Tracking).
  - **Body**: 'JetBrains Mono' or 'DM Mono' (Monospace, highly readable).
</idea>

  **Design Movement**: Ethereal Glassmorphism (Aurora UI)
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Depth & Layering**: Using blur, transparency, and shadows to create a sense of deep space.
  2. **Fluidity**: Organic shapes and gradients that move and evolve.
  3. **Light & Shadow**: Interfaces that feel like they are interacting with a light source.
  4. **Softness**: Rounded corners, smooth transitions, and gentle contrasts.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Dark Mode Base**: Deep void colors (Midnight Blue #0f172a, Deep Purple #1e1b4b).
  - **Aurora Gradients**: Soft, moving gradients of Cyan, Magenta, and Violet that act as background lighting.
  - **Intent**: To evoke creativity, innovation, and a "future-forward" mindset.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Floating Cards**: Content contained in semi-transparent, blurred cards floating above the background.
  - **Centralized Focus**: Key content centered with ample breathing room, guiding the eye naturally.
  - **Overlapping Elements**: Sections that slightly overlap to create continuity.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Glass Panes**: Backdrop-filter: blur() heavily used on containers.
  - **Glowing Borders**: Borders that fade in/out or have a gradient stroke.
  - **Orbital Backgrounds**: Animated glowing orbs moving slowly in the background.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Magnetic Hover**: Elements that gently pull towards the cursor.
  - **Glow Effects**: Elements light up (inner glow) when interacted with.

  **Animation**:
  - **Fluid Motion**: Slow, ease-in-out transitions for all movements.
  - **Parallax**: Background elements moving at different speeds than foreground content.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: 'Outfit' or 'Plus Jakarta Sans' (Geometric, clean, friendly).
  - **Body**: 'Inter' or 'Satoshi' (Neutral, legible).

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Editorial Minimalist (Digital Magazine)
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Content First**: The work itself is the hero; the interface recedes.
  2. **Typographic Hierarchy**: Using size and weight to guide the eye, rather than boxes and borders.
  3. **Structured Whitespace**: Using negative space to group and separate content.
  4. **Refined Elegance**: A focus on subtle details and high-quality assets.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Warm Neutral**: Off-white/Cream (#FDFBF7) or Warm Grey (#1C1C1C) background.
  - **Accent**: A single, sophisticated accent color like Burnt Sienna (#E07A5F) or Deep Emerald (#2A9D8F).
  - **Intent**: To convey professionalism, maturity, and a focus on quality.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Split Screen**: Text on one side, visuals on the other (sticky scrolling).
  - **Large Typography**: Oversized headings that act as graphic elements.
  - **Grid-Breaking**: Images or text that intentionally break the grid alignment for visual interest.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Serif Headings**: Elegant serif fonts for titles to add character.
  - **Thin Lines**: Hairline dividers (1px) to separate sections.
  - **Large Imagery**: Full-width or large aspect ratio images for project previews.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Smooth Scrolling**: Inertia-based scrolling feel.
  - **Image Reveal**: Images that slide or fade in as they enter the viewport.

  **Animation**:
  - **Fade & Slide**: Elements gently fade in and slide up as you scroll.
  - **Text Reveal**: Words or lines revealing themselves in sequence.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: 'Playfair Display' or 'Instrument Serif' (Elegant, high contrast).
  - **Body**: 'General Sans' or 'Switzer' (Clean, modern sans-serif).
</idea>
